Tic Tac Toe

Do you want to play a game?

​
Your Move Player 1

Steps

  • Step 1Build a 3X3 board in HTML and CSS. You could do this with either a table element, or a collection of divs. Both have their advantages and disadvantages.
  • Step 2 Attach a touchend listener to the board. When a cell is touched it should add either an 'X or an 'O', depending on whether it is player 1 or player 2's turn.
  • Step 3Add a reset button.. When touched the reset button should remove all of the marks from the board.
